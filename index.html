About
Hello! I am a Masters Student at the University of Texas at Dallas, advised by Vincent Ng. I obtained my B.E. degree in Computer Science at CBIT, where I was to be advised by Srujana Inturi. Before my master's, I was a software engineer at J.P Morgan Chase & C.o, where I worked on AWS EMR controller, as an infrastructure engineer and solutions architect enabling clients to migrate to EMR for their Big Data projects.
My primary research focus is to improve efficiency and quantify uncertainty in language models. Concretely, my research interests include: (i) developing LLM Evaluation frameworks (e.g., LLM-as-a-Judge, Meta-Evaluation) that systematically identify what specific capabilities language models lack and (ii) looping back the insights acquired from evaluation to train stronger LMs for Weak-to-Strong generalization (e.g., reward overoptimization, training with verbal feedback).
